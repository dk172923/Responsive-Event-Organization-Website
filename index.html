about us

Welcome to TecFest2k24, the exclusive student-led technical extravaganza at Tagore Engineering College (TEC), happening on March 26, 2024. TecFest2k24 is the ultimate showcase of student innovation and collaboration.

coding competitions to paper presentations, students test their skills in a thrilling atmosphere of friendly rivalry. Beyond the technical sphere, TecFest2k24 offers a range of engaging non-technical events, providing students with opportunities to unwind, socialize, and foster connections with their peers.

Join us at TecFest2k24 as we celebrate the passion, talent, and ingenuity of TEC's student community. Together, let's explore new frontiers, spark innovation, and inspire the next generation of technological pioneers.
