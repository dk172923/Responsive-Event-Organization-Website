about us

Welcome to TecFest2k24 at Tagore Engineering College (TEC) conducted by Computer Science and engineering department, on March 26, 2024. From epic coding competitions to paper presentations, where students can showcase their innovation and collaboration. Join us for a thrilling atmosphere of friendly rivalry and engaging non-technical events. Let's celebrate TEC's student community and inspire the next generation of technological pioneers.

technical events

Code Clash

Welcome to Code Clash, where coding mastery shines! Engage in an exhilarating cooperative challenge with your teammate as you navigate coding puzzles. Seamlessly switch roles and demonstrate collective skills to conquer each round. Ready to clash and emerge victorious? Join us now and showcase your coding expertise!

Paper Presentation

Embark on the journey of paper presentations, where knowledge shines bright! Delve into your research, sharing its purpose, methodology, and exciting findings with clarity. Enrich your talk with compelling visuals, guiding your audience through your discoveries. Brace yourself for engaging discussions, as minds collide and curiosity ignites.






Tech Quiz Trivia

Welcome to Tech Quest Trivia, where students face off in an electrifying quiz competition! Join us for a thrilling event filled with brain-teasing questions and fierce competition. Are you ready to claim the title of quiz champion? Let the battle begin!

Web Blitz

Get ready for the excitement of Web Blitz, the ultimate web design competition! Witness peers' coding and design skills in an electrifying showdown. Join us for a thrilling display of creativity. Don't miss out on this high-stakes battle of web design mastery!

non-technical events

treasure hunt

Join the exhilarating Treasure Hunt at our college event! Navigate through clues and obstacles, uncovering hidden treasures. Test your wit and teamwork in this thrilling quest for glory. Gather your friends and embark on an unforgettable adventure!

crickchase carnival

Experience the adrenaline of CrickChase Carnival, our Box Cricket Extravaganza! Form your team of three and dive into an exhilarating cricket adventure. With limited space and fast-paced action, get ready for a thrilling experience like no other. Get ready to chase!

Connection club

Welcome to Connections Game! Uncover Hidden Links, Sharpening Your Wit and Strategy. Engage in this Thought-Provoking Pursuit, Unraveling Mysteries with Clever Associations. Join Us for an Exhilarating and Enlightening Journey Through Intriguing Connections!
