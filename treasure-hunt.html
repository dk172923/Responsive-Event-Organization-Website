Treasure Hunt

Embark on the Treasure Hunt Trio, a 3-round adventure where teams of three solve clues, tackle puzzles, and navigate obstacles to find hidden treasures. Participants must rely on teamwork and strategy, with no running allowed. Will your team emerge victorious in this epic quest for treasure?

Rules:

  • All teams must have 3 members.
  • If number of teams is more than 10, then shortlisting will be based on quiz.
  • Original code creation is mandatory; plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
  • Teams must stay together during the Treasure Hunt.
  • Exchanging hints or interfering with other teams will lead to disqualification.
