CrickChase Carnival

Welcome to CrickChase Carnival, our Box Cricket Extravaganza! Gather your team of three for a thrilling cricket experience. With a limited playing area and a defined set of rules, this fast-paced event promises non-stop excitement. Get ready to showcase your cricket skills and compete against other teams in the ultimate battle for victory!

Rules:

  • Per team must consists of 4 members.
  • Every match is a knockout stage.
  • Hitting a six consider as a wicket.
  • Per match consists of 5 overs.
  • legside and offside are considered as 2G
  • Straight is considered as 4
