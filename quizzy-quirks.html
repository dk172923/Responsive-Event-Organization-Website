Tech Quiz Trivia

Tech Quiz Trivia – The Solo Master, with two rounds, Where Participant who Answers Maximum number of Question within short time will be Qualified to Second Round. The Winner and Runner will be awarded with cash prize. Solo Master depends on their own knowledge. Are you ready to seize your moment and claim the title of Solo Master?

Rules:

  • Event is divided into two rounds, with each participant competing individually.
  • Round 1 consists of 15 questions on Shuffled Technical Words. Round 2 presents 15 medium-level questions.
  • The decisions of the quiz master are considered final.
  • Participants are strictly prohibited from accessing the internet for answers.
  • Cash prizes of ₹1000 for the winners and ₹500 for the runners-up will be awarded.
